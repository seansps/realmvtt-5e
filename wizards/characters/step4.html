Select your Character's Species.

All available Species in the current Campaign will be shown in the dropdown below.

After completing this step, select Spells by dragging them from the Spells Compendium to your Actions tab.

Equipment can be added by dragging Items from the Items Compendium to your Inventory tab.

Be sure to review your chosen Species and Background's Origin Feat as you may also have additional Skills or Abilities that you need to add manually as a result of these selections.

From the Notes tab, you can set your Alignment, Deity, and other relevant details.
